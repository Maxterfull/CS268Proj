Banana Split

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana, peeled and split lengthwise
  • 3 scoops of ice cream (vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry)
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Strawberry syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Chopped nuts (e.g., peanuts or walnuts)
  • Maraschino cherry

Instructions:

  1. Place the split banana halves in a long serving dish.
  2. Add the three scoops of ice cream between the banana halves: vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry.
  3. Drizzle chocolate syrup over the chocolate ice cream scoop and strawberry syrup over the strawberry ice cream scoop.
  4. Top each scoop of ice cream with a dollop of whipped cream.
  5. Sprinkle chopped nuts over the whipped cream and ice cream.
  6. Place a maraschino cherry on top of the whipped cream of each ice cream scoop.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your banana split!

Enjoy your delicious banana split!

Creamy Polenta

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup whole milk
  • 2 cup water
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 cup polenta or yellow cornmeal
  • 4 teaspoon unsalted butter
  • 1.25 cup marmesan cheese
  • 1 package of cherry tomatoes
  • Salt + pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, bring milk, water, and salt to a boil.
  2. In a baking dish, place the tomatoes, sprinkle with a splash of olive oil and season with salt and pepper, cook for 35 to 45 minutes.
  3. Gradually whisk in polenta.
  4. Reduce heat to low, and cover.
  5. Cook 20 minutes, until creamy, lifting the lid and stirring every 3 or 4 minutes to prevent sticking.
  6. Remove pot from stove, stir in pepper and butter. Gradually stir in cheese.

Enjoy your delicious polenta and tomatoes!
